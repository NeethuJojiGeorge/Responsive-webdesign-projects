Bob Ross

The man who brought the joy in painting

Here's a timeline of Bob Ross's life:

  • 1942 - Born in Daytona Beach, Florida on October 29.
  • 1960 - Enlisted in the United States Air Force and served as a medical records technician.
  • 1960 - While stationed in Alaska, see's "The Magic of Oil Painting", hosted by German painter Bill Alexander, and studies the wet-on-wet oil painting technique under him.
  • 1981 - Retires from the Air Force with a rank of Master Sergeant.
  • 1983 - The Joy of Painting airs its first episode on January 11.
  • 1994 - The Joy of Painting airs its final episode on May 17, due to Ross being diagnosed with lymphoma.
  • 1995 - Dies at the age of 52 on July 4, from lymphoma.

"I think there's an artist hidden at the bottom of every single one of us."

-- Bob Ross
